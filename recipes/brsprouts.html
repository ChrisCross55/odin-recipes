Air Fried Brussel Sprouts

A crispy bite-sized head of lettuce

Brussel sprouts can be cooked a variety of different ways. My favorite way to eat them is air fried. The crispy edges, soft center, and ability to pair nicely with many different flavors makes this recipe a must try.

Ingredients

  • Half a pound of brussel sprouts
  • olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Wash, dry, and halve brussel sprouts
  2. Set air fryer to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  3. In a medium bowl, add brussel sprouts, a splash of olive oil and vinegar , and spices to taste.
  4. Toss brussel sprouts to evenly coat and add to air fryer basket
  5. Place in air fryer and cook for 15-25min or until browned to your taste
